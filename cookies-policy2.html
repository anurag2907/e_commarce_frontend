Cookies Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zyvra Jewelry's Cookies Policy. This policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analyzing website traffic
  • Personalizing content and ads
  • Enabling certain functionalities (e.g., shopping cart, login sessions)

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core features like user authentication and shopping cart functionality.

Performance & Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous data to improve performance.

Functional Cookies

Enhance your experience by remembering preferences (e.g., language settings, font size).

Marketing & Advertising Cookies

Track browsing habits to deliver personalized ads. They may be set by third-party services.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Browser Controls:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy, Search & Services > Cookies

Third-Party Opt-Outs:

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Revised" date.
